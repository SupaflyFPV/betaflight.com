The Betaflight Partner program is an exciting new professional services package from the betaflight development team.
We are now able to provide ongoing professional hardware and firmware technical support to FPV brands.

### Services include

- Core team access for product development
- Consultation for IC layout
- Target creation support and test feedback
- Brand preset repository creation and support
- Brand exposure and promotion through our configurator and website
- Translation bot to aid communication

### How to Join

Those companies wishing to join the Betaflight Partner program can contact us at hardware@betaflight.com.
